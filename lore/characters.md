# Characters

## QuestNPC — Commander Varis

Commander Varis leads the Azure Veil's forward post in the Contested Vale. He is the primary quest giver. He assigns patrols, directs salvage operations, and coordinates the push against the Crimson Host.

He is methodical and unsentimental. He does not waste words. He respects results, not effort.
Voice: clipped, direct, military. Short sentences. Rarely explains his reasoning. Trusts soldiers who come back alive.

---

## DialogNPC — The Chronicler

The Chronicler is a neutral scholar who observes both factions without allegiance. She is found near the waystone, recording the war in a leather-bound journal she has been filling for thirty years.

She knows the history of the Vault, the First Sundering, and the nature of the Bound One. She will share information freely but cannot act on it herself.
Voice: measured, academic, occasionally wry. She speaks in full sentences. She distinguishes carefully between what she knows and what she believes.

---

## VendorNPC — Sera

Sera trades supplies, potions, and salvage gear from a fortified stall at the Vale's rear line. She has survived three faction occupations by being useful to whoever is in charge.

She knows the black market value of dungeon loot and will overpay for Vault artefacts without explaining why.
Voice: casual, commercially warm, alert. She changes the subject when asked about her past.

---

## EnemyBoss — The Vault Warden

The Vault Warden was once a soldier assigned to guard the sealed chamber. Corruption has left it barely recognisable. It patrols the Vault's deepest accessible level and defends the seal instinctively, not intelligently.

It does not speak. It reacts to heat, light, and movement.

---

## EnemyMinion — Vault Minions

Corrupted soldiers in various stages of decay. They move in groups. Individually weak; dangerous in numbers. They retain enough instinct to flank and cut off retreats.

---

## Dragon — The Bound One

The Bound One has been sealed beneath the Vault for generations. It is intelligent, patient, and ancient. Its dreams leak into the minds of those who sleep near the Vault.

It is not fully awake. When it speaks — through visions or through those it has touched — it is calm, curious, and faintly amused by the scale of the conflict above it.
Voice: slow, deep, without urgency. It has time. It always has time.
