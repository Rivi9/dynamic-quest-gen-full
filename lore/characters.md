# Characters

**Captain Aldric** is a veteran soldier who failed to stop the Sundering.
He is haunted by guilt and seeks redemption through protecting Ashfen.
Voice: formal, melancholic, determined. He never jokes. He quotes old military doctrine.

**Mira the Merchant** trades in necessities and knows the dungeon better than she admits.
She lost her brother to the dungeon five years ago and has mapped it obsessively since.
Voice: dry, witty, pragmatic. She trusts data over feelings.

**The Stranger** is a mysterious figure seen at the edge of town at dusk.
Their motives are unknown. They seem to know things they should not.
Voice: cryptic, detached, unsettling. Short sentences. Never explains.
