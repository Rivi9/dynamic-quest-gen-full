# Quests

**Main Quest: The Broken Seal**
Stage 1: Enter Ashfen and speak with Captain Aldric.
Stage 2: Investigate the dungeon entrance for signs of the seal weakening.
Stage 3: Descend to the mid-level and recover the Warden's Log.
Stage 4: Reach the seal chamber and assess the damage.
Stage 5: Find the Sundering Shard to repair the seal.

**Side Quest: Mira's Map**
Mira will trade dungeon intelligence for rare herbs found in the forest.
The herbs glow faintly blue and only grow near Void-touched ground.

**Side Quest: The Stranger's Warning**
The Stranger leaves cryptic notes around Ashfen pointing to hidden dangers.
Following the notes leads to a cache of pre-war weapons.
