# Quests

## KillThingsQuest — Clear the Advance

Commander Varis needs the Crimson Host's forward units pushed back from the Vale's centre.
Objective: eliminate Red faction soldiers (RedFighter, RedArcher, RedWarrior, RedMage) in the contested zone.
Context: the Crimson Host has advanced further than expected. The Azure Veil's supply line is at risk.

---

## CollectionQuest — Salvage the Fallen

Weapons and supplies lost in the fighting need to be recovered before the Crimson Host finds them.
Objective: collect dropped items from the battlefield.
Context: Azure Veil equipment is expensive to replace. Sera at the supply post will accept salvage.

---

## DialogObjectiveQuest — Consult the Chronicler

The Chronicler near the waystone has information about the Vault's seal.
Objective: speak with the Chronicler (DialogNPC) to learn the history of the First Sundering.
Context: Varis needs to know whether the Vault can be safely entered before committing soldiers.

---

## VisitZoneQuest — Descend to the Vault

Scout the upper halls of the Vault and report what is inside.
Objective: enter FeaturesDemoDungeon and reach the first checkpoint.
Context: neither faction has sent a full force into the Vault yet. Intelligence is needed.

---

## UseInteractableQuest — Activate the Waystone

The Vale's ancient waystone may still function as a rally point if reactivated.
Objective: interact with the waystone in the Contested Vale.
Context: a functioning waystone would allow faster troop movement across the Vale.

---

## RepeatableQuest / Repeatable2StepQuest / Repeatable3StepQuest — Patrol Duty

Ongoing patrol assignments in the Contested Vale. Varis rotates soldiers through patrol routes to keep the Crimson Host from establishing permanent positions.
Objective: complete patrol checkpoints in the Vale.
Context: patrol strength determines how much ground the Azure Veil holds each day.

---

## FinishAnotherQuestQuest — Vault Clearance

Before the Azure Veil will commit to a full assault on the sealed chamber, the upper Vault halls must be cleared.
Prerequisite: complete the descent scout (VisitZoneQuest) first.
Objective: defeat EnemyMinion units in the upper Vault halls.
Context: clearing the approach removes the risk of being flanked from behind during the assault.

---

## ChooseRewardQuest — Varis's Favour

Varis rewards those who have proven themselves with a choice of commendations: equipment, supplies, or a favour owed.
Objective: complete a series of tasks for the Azure Veil.
Context: faction reputation matters. Higher standing with the Azure Veil unlocks better equipment from Sera.

---

## StatusEffectQuest / UseAbilityQuest / LearnAbilityQuest / LearnTradeSkillQuest — Training Orders

Varis requires soldiers to demonstrate combat readiness before assignment to the Vault.
Objective: use a specific ability, learn a skill, or apply a status effect in combat.
Context: unprepared soldiers inside the Vault become a liability. Varis does not send the untrained underground.
