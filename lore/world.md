# World Lore

The kingdom of Eryndal has fallen into shadow after the Sundering War.
Ancient seals that once contained the Void-touched creatures have crumbled.

The Thornwood Dungeon was once a royal armory but is now overrun by creatures drawn to the broken seal at its deepest level.
The dungeon's three floors each grow darker: the entry hall is patrolled by corrupted guards, the mid-level holds the beast pens, and the seal chamber waits at the bottom.

The village of Ashfen sits at the dungeon's entrance, its population dwindled to a handful of survivors who refuse to abandon their ancestral home.
Ashfen's well is said to grant visions to those who drink from it at midnight.

The Sundering War ended three decades ago when the last Void-touched titan was sealed beneath Thornwood.
