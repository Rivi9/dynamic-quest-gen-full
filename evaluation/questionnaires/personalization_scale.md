# Custom Narrative Personalization Scale (NPS-3)

*Developed for this study. Three items on a 7-point Likert scale.*

**Scale:** 1 = Strongly Disagree … 7 = Strongly Agree

---

## Instructions

*Please rate your agreement with each statement about the game's story and narrative.*

| # | Item |
|---|------|
| 1 | The story felt tailored to how I was playing. |
| 2 | The narrative responded to my choices and actions. |
| 3 | The game seemed to understand what kind of player I am. |

---

## Scoring

- **NPS-3 Score** = Mean of items 1–3 (range 1–7)
- Higher = greater perceived personalization
- Compute Cronbach's α; if α < 0.70, report as separate items

---

## Pilot Testing Note

Pilot with n=3 before main study to check item comprehension.
