# miniPXI — Player Experience Inventory (Short Form)

**Citation:** Vornhagen, J. B., Thue, D., & Göbel, S. (2022). miniPXI: Development and Validation of an Eleven-Item Measure of Player Experience. *Proc. ACM CHI* 2022.
DOI: https://doi.org/10.1145/3491102.3502048

**Scale:** 1 = Strongly Disagree … 7 = Strongly Agree

---

## Instructions

*The following statements describe how you felt while playing. Please rate your agreement.*

| # | Item | Scale |
|---|------|-------|
| 1 | I felt in control of my character/avatar | 1–7 |
| 2 | I felt a sense of mastery when completing challenges | 1–7 |
| 3 | I found the game world interesting to explore | 1–7 |
| 4 | I was curious about what would happen next in the game | 1–7 |
| 5 | I felt like the game narrative pulled me in | 1–7 |
| 6 | I had a sense of presence in the game world | 1–7 |
| 7 | I felt free to make meaningful choices | 1–7 |
| 8 | The game felt meaningful to me | 1–7 |
| 9 | I had positive emotions while playing | 1–7 |
| 10 | I felt excited during the game | 1–7 |
| 11 | Overall, I enjoyed playing this game | 1–7 |

---

## Subscale Mapping

| Construct | Items |
|-----------|-------|
| Mastery | 1, 2 |
| Curiosity | 3, 4 |
| Immersion | 5, 6 |
| Autonomy | 7, 8 |
| Meaning | 8 |
| Positive Affect | 9, 10, 11 |

---

## Notes for Analysis

- Use total score (mean of all 11 items) as a holistic player experience index
- Also report per-subscale scores
- Cronbach's α target ≥ 0.70
